## âœï¸ Writing Module (NEW!)

### Flow:
1. Click "Writing" card
2. Browse 5 writing prompts:
   - Task 1 Academic (graphs/charts)
   - Task 1 General (letters)
   - Task 2 (essays)
3. Click "Start Writing"
4. **Writing Editor Opens:**
   - Large text area for your essay
   - Live word counter (color-coded)
   - Countdown timer
   - Auto-save every 30 seconds
   - Writing tips panel on the right
   - Character/paragraph/time stats
5. Write your essay
6. Click "Submit for Feedback"
7. **AI Analyzes Your Writing** (takes 10-30 seconds)
8. **Results Page Shows:**
   - Overall Band Score (big display)
   - 4 Criteria Scores:
     - Task Achievement
     - Coherence & Cohesion
     - Lexical Resource
     - Grammatical Range
   - Detailed feedback for each criterion:
     - Overall assessment
     - Strengths (green boxes)
     - Areas for improvement (blue boxes)
   - Vocabulary Analysis:
     - Advanced vocabulary you used (green tags)
     - Overused words (yellow tags)
     - Suggestions for improvement
   - Grammar Corrections:
     - Each error shown
     - Correction provided
     - Explanation given
   - Your full essay displayed
9. Options:
   - Write Another Essay
   - View Model Answer (if available)
   - Back to Dashboard
